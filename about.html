About Ashten

My Story

I’m just your regular, average, former opera singer turned user experience designer with years of experience with cross-functional, agile product development teams. I’m passionate about creating solutions to problems that are transparent, unpretentious and empower the user to live their best lives. I have experience with influencing and collaborating on product strategies so that they reflect problem spaces which are solvable, measurable and actionable, the goal being that teams can feel empowered to solve meaningful problems and clearly define success. I’ve also had the opportunity to craft design visions that help orient teams around an entire product ecosystem so they can maintain focus yet are reminded to major less in the minors.
